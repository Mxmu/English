Be动词的否定
在am,is,are,was,were后面加Not，缩略式am not, isn't,aren't,wasn't,weren't.

1.The man isn't back.  那个男人没回来。
2.I am not back.  我没有回来。
3.They aren't back.  他们没有回来。
4.He wasn't back. 他没有回来。
5.They weren't back. 他们没有回来。

使用be动词提问和回答
提问：be 动词放到句首，其他顺序不变。
-Is he a teacher?
 他是一名老师吗？
-Yes,he is./No,he isn't.

-Are you a teacher?
你是一名老师吗？
-Yes,I am./No,I am not.

-Were they teachers?
他们以前是老师吗？
-Yes,they were./No,they weren't.

Be动词的练习
1.他是医生吗？ (doctor）
 Is he a doctor?
  不，他不是。
  No,he isn't.

2.他们昨天在教室吗？
  (yesterday,classroom)
 Were they in the classroom yesterday?
  是的，他们在。
 Yes,they were.

3.他们昨天不在教室。
 They weren't in the classroom yesterday.
