反身代词，通常作宾语或者同位语
myself              yourselves
yourself            ourselves
herself              themselves.
himself
itself

反身代词用法
1.Please help yourself to some fish.
   
2.We enjoyed ourselves last night.
   我们昨天晚上玩得很愉快

3.The thing itself is not important.
   同位语

练习
1.Take good care of (yourself).

2.She gained control of (herself).
